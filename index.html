Smoking Carribean Sunset

  1. Hickory Wood Chips
  2. Splash of Grenadine
  3. 0.5 oz Lemon Juice
  4. 1 oz Triple Sec
  5. 2 oz Coconut Rum
  6. 3 oz Pineapple Juice
Cover the bottom of the glass in a thin layer of grenadine. Add the rest of the liquid ingredients to a shaker w/ ice and shake. Fill glass halfway w/ ice. Layer the drink onto the grenadine with a strainer. Fill w/ ice and smoke w/ hickory.
